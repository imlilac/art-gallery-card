Vincent Willem van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch painter who lived in the 19th century. He is renowned for his contributions to the post-impressionist art movement.
Van Gogh's artistic style was characterized by vibrant colors and expressive brushwork. He aimed to convey emotions and inner experiences through his paintings.
